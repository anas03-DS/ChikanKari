About Us

Our story, mission and the people behind Chikankari Artisans

Our Story

Chikankari Artisans was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to preserve the centuries-old tradition of Chikankari embroidery while ensuring fair livelihoods for the artisans who create these magnificent pieces.

What began as a small initiative to help connect a handful of talented artisans from Lucknow with potential customers has now grown into a thriving platform that supports over 200 artisans from across northern India.

Our founder, Ayesha Sharma, was inspired by her grandmother's collection of Chikankari work and the stories of the artisans who created them. After witnessing the challenges faced by these skilled craftspeople in the modern market, she decided to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and contemporary consumers.
