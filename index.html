About Chikankari

Chikankari is a delicate and traditional hand embroidery originating from Lucknow, India. This exquisite craft involves creating intricate patterns on fabric using white thread on colorful backgrounds.

Our platform connects you directly with skilled young artisans who are keeping this traditional art alive while earning fair compensation for their craftsmanship.
