Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Domestic shipping within India typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on your location. All orders are processed within 24-48 hours of payment confirmation.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused items in their original packaging. Custom-made items cannot be returned unless there's a manufacturing defect. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How can I become an artisan on your platform?

To join as an artisan, you need to fill out the application form on our "Artisans" page. Our team will review your application, and if approved, we'll schedule an interview and sample evaluation. The process typically takes 1-2 weeks.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 25 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on your location. You can see the exact shipping cost during checkout after entering your address.

How do I care for my Chikankari products?

For best results, hand wash your Chikankari items in cold water with mild detergent. Avoid bleach or strong chemicals. Let them air dry in shade, and iron on medium heat while the fabric is still slightly damp. Each product comes with specific care instructions.
