Product Description

This handcrafted Chikankari Kurta is a testament to the rich cultural heritage of Lucknow's traditional embroidery. Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who have inherited this craft through generations.

The intricate embroidery features a blend of 36 different stitches that create a three-dimensional effect on the fabric. The elegant white thread work on cool blue cotton fabric showcases classic Chikankari motifs including paisley patterns, floral designs, and geometric shapes.

This versatile piece can be paired with cotton pants or palazzos for a casual day look or dressed up with silk bottoms and statement jewelry for special occasions.
